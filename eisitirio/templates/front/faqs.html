Last Updated 8th September 2019

Account Registration

  • Alumni Release opens at noon Tuesday 15 October 2019.
  • Student Release opens at noon on Thursday 31 October 2019.

Ticket payment

  • Tickets must be paid for within 24 hours.
    • If tickets are not paid for within 24 hours, they will expire and be allocated to the next person on the waiting list.
    • If your tickets expire due to non-payment, we will be unable to re-allocate tickets to you.
  • Tickets may be paid for by credit/debit card, or, if you are a current Keble student, by Battels.
    • We do not support payment by American Express (Amex) cards.
  • If your ticket is not marked as paid, please contact the ticketing officer as soon as possible with proof of purchase.

Waiting List

  • If you are unable to purchase tickets during the general release, you have the option to join the waiting list.
  • If extra tickets are released, or if other users cancel their tickets, the extra availability will be allocated to people on the waiting list.
  • Tickets will only be allocated in the blocks in which they are requested.
    • If you join the waiting list for 4 tickets, you will not be allocated tickets until there are 4 tickets available.

Cancellations, Refunds and Resales

  • Refunds of tickets purchased by card will only be offered in exceptional circumstances, up until 21 April 2020 and will incur an admin fee of £10 per ticket. Refunds of tickets on and after 22 April 2020 will incur an admin fee of £20 per tickt. No ticket will be refunded after 1 May 2020.
  • Refunds of tickets purchased through Battles will only be offered until the end of Hilary term.
  • Tickets may not be resold under any circumstances.
  • Any tickets found to have been resold will be cancelled, and both the seller and purchaser will be refused entry to the ball.

Guests

  • Each alumnus can purchase up to 10 tickets.
  • All guests are permitted to attend the Ball provided that they are over the age of 18 years old on or after 27 June 2020.
  • Each guest who has had a ticket purchased on their behalf will have to register their own account on the Keble Ball website by 1 May 2020. They can then clain their ticket using the unique ticket claim code shared by the ticket purchaser.

Tickets & Account Photo

  • Once you have purchased your ticket, you are entirely responsible for it. You may not share the QR Code related to your ticket.
  • Once you are handed your wristband, you are entirely responsible for it.
    • Keble Ball will not be able to replace or refund any lost, stolen or damaged wristbands.
  • Your account must have an identifiable photo of yourself for security purposes.
    • If your photo is not currently an identifiable headshot it must be changed. This can be done here.
    • An identifiable headshot must be uploaded before you buy your ticket.
    • Any changes to your photo after that will be subject to an administration charge.
  • Keble Ball reserves the right to refuse you entrance at the gate if your account photo is not easily identifiable as you.
  • Valid photo ID must be presented when asked for at the gate.
    • Examples of valid ID includes University ID Cards (Bod Cards), driving licenses, passports etc.
    • Keble Ball reserves the right to refuse you entrance if you cannot present valid ID.

On the night

  • You will need to arrive before 9pm, when gates close.
  • A strict no re-admittance policy will be operating on the night of the Ball, so please bring jackets and flat shoes with you.
  • Wristbands must be worn at all times during the Ball.
    • Failure to wear your wristband will result in you being removed from the Ball.
  • You must present your wristband for inspection when asked by any member of security staff or the Keble Ball Committee.
    • Failure to present your wristband when asked will result in you being removed from the Ball.
